This Restaurant Sales and Food Cost Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the restaurant's business performance.It displays key KPIs such as Total Revenue, Total Orders, Total Items Sold, Average Order Value, Total Profit, and Food Cost %. The dashboard also includes interactive slicers to filter data by month, category, customer type, and payment method, along with charts showing monthly revenue, revenue by category, top and bottom selling items, branch-wise revenue, orders by day of the week, payment methods, customer ratings, and sales trends. It helps users monitor sales performance, analyze customer behavior, and make informed business decisions.
